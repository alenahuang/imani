Imani Radio on the Air

We are host to a bi-weekly radio program on WUBS 89.7 FM faith-based community channel. “Truth Tellin’ Time” airs Tuesdays, 4:00 pm-5:00 pm. The program is live and accepts call-ins from the listening audience. The purpose of this project is to promote respectful dialogue around challenging topics. We are host to a monthly television broadcast of “Truth Tellin’ Time” on WNIT community access channel.
